About Thirukkural

One of the most revered works of Tamil literature, spanning ethics, politics, and personal life.

அறத்துப்பால் · Virtue

380 kurals on moral and spiritual living — righteousness, family life, asceticism, and fate. The foundation of a virtuous life.

பொருட்பால் · Wealth

700 kurals on polity, governance, diplomacy, and the conduct of leaders. Practical wisdom for rulers and citizens alike.

காமத்துப்பால் · Love

250 kurals on human love and relationships — from courtship to the pain of separation, expressed with poetic beauty.

Thiruvalluvar

The legendary Tamil poet-saint who composed these 1330 couplets. Each kural is just two lines, yet carries profound universal truth.
